[Manufacturing Unit]
~Nom du produit~
The type of product that the unit is currently manufacturing.^
~Stock de produits~
Cette zone indique le stock du produit dans l'unité. Chaque boîte représente une cargaison du produit. Chaque cargaison peut stocker un certain nombre d'unités du produit.^ 
~Stock de matières premieres~
The stocks of raw materials are represented by a row of green bars here. The length of each bar indicates the quantity of the raw material stocked in the unit. During a manufacturing process, raw materials must first go into a manufacturing unit and then converted into products. The manufacturing process is suspended whenever any of its required raw materials is lacking. Thus, to have the unit operating at maximum efficiency, you must provide a consistent supply of raw materials.^
~Utilisation~
Cette barre marron montre l'utilisation de l'unité. Si cette barre atteint sa pleine longueur, l'unité a atteint sa pleine capacité. It can manufacture no more goods even if there is additional supply and demand. On the other hand, if this bar is short, this indicates that its facilities and resources are not being used effectively. This might contribute to the firm's low profitability.
